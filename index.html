Tax Day Floods Revisited

About this comparison

Two floods, one map

In April 2016, the Tax Day storm brought historic flooding to parts of the Houston region. This map compares typical flood exposure in Clear Creek with what that 2016 Tax Day flood would look like if transposed here.

  • Historic — Modeled depths for how this area usually floods.
  • Transposed — Depths if the 2016 Tax Day flood pattern were applied here.
